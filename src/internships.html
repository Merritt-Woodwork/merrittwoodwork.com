Our paid internship opportunities offer a chance for students and recent graduates to learn both the craft and business of fine woodworking. Our interns gain hands-on experience under the guidance of the best in the industry leading to better career opportunities in the future.

We count on our relationships with colleges, universities and art institutes to identify the talent we need. Our interns are important to the growth and success of our company.
