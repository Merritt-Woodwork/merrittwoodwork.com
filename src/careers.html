Culture

Merritt employees transform architectural dreams into realities. The Merritt process is a complex collaboration of skilled individuals. From carpenters to engineers to project managers, everyone at Merritt contributes to our renowned and in-demand finished product.

As a growth-focused, international company, Merritt operates multiple offices throughout the U.S., and has recently expanded beyond these borders into Germany. We are always in search of individuals who want to propel themselves and this organization into the future. Forward thinkers and motivational leaders are our kind of people.

Merritt encourages personal growth and career development at all levels. We maintain a culture that embraces change, offers visibility to senior management and provides the opportunity to work with professional artisans, technicians and industry experts.

Social events such as company picnics, sport outings, holiday parties, volunteer opportunities and more bring us together for fun and friendship.

Contact us if you are looking to become part of a winning team, see your contribution make a difference and grow both yourself and the organization in the process.

Benefits

We provide a comprehensive benefits program that allows you the opportunity to choose the type of coverage you want. Our coverage includes medical, dental, vision, disability, life insurance, health savings account, flexible spending accounts, and other programs that provide value and peace of mind to our employees. We are committed to helping our employees lead healthy lifestyles by providing an on-site wellness coach and free fitness center memberships.
